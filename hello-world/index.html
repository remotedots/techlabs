Hello World! Why TechLabs Rotterdam Imagine a world full of tech-savvy individuals who approach the challenges of our time with a digital and entrepreneurial mindset. To achieve this, TechLabs Rotterdam offers a free blended learning platform combining Online Learning, Project Work, and TechLabs Community Events to improve your coding skills! Learning is more effective (and fun) in a community of likeminded and ambitious people. Let’s learn together and gain access to a network of experienced mentors from universities, start-ups and corporations. You can find more informations _here_. About Rotterdam Known as the “gateway to Europe” due to its large seaport, Rotterdam is a cultural melting pot with over 170 nationalities. Famous for its modern architecture as well as inventing ‘Kapsalon’, Rotterdam is home to many internationally renowned knowledge institutions and universities. With so much diversity, innovation and know-how in one place, it’s no wonder this city is developing into a major European tech & start-up hub with more than 500 tech start-ups (and counting). We believe Rotterdam is one of the best places to enter the Tech World– independent of your prior knowledge.
